Beautifully defined brows draw attention to the eyes. With a clean brush, comb the hairs up, then fill in the "gaps" between them with shadows that repeat the color of the eyebrow shade. Move from the base of the eyebrow to the "tail".

If the density of the eyebrows suits you, use a transparent gel to simply lay and fix the hairs. Suitable, for example, control freak from nix professional makeup.

To make the look deep, distribute light shadows over the entire surface of the eyelid, and apply dark shadows in the crease, from the same color scheme-properly blend. This make-up trick will provide a 3D effect to your makeup.

Perhaps one of the most important steps in makeup is choosing a shade that matches the color of your eyes. A universal rule: choose the shadow colors that are opposite to the color of your eyes on the color wheel. Thus, for blue eyes, shades of bronze and copper are perfect, for green and brown – purple and taupe. Among other things, pay attention to the skin tone: the darker it is, the warmer shades of shadows should be chosen.
